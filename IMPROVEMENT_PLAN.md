## Executive Summary

**Traffic Data (from Microsoft Clarity):**
- Top referrers: www.hmz.technology (74), Google (54), Direct (35), ChatGPT (14)

High traffic, low conversion. Visitors are coming but not submitting the contact form.

1. Form has too many fields (6+ fields = friction)

4. No interactive ROI calculator
5. Generic CTAs ("Claim Your Free Audit")

7. No exit-intent capture
8. Pricing not transparent
9. No real-time social proof
10. Form buried at bottom of page
11. No WhatsApp widget for instant contact
12. Missing urgency triggers that feel authentic

# CATEGORY A: CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION (Phases 1-25)

## Phase 1: Simplify Contact Form
**Priority: CRITICAL**
**Expected Impact: +30-50% form submissions**

Current form fields (6):
- Name (required)
- Email (required)
- Phone/WhatsApp (required)
- Company Name
- Service Selection
- Message (required)

**Action:**
Reduce to 3 essential fields:
- Name
- Email OR WhatsApp (choose one)
- "Tell us briefly what you need"

**Research backing:** Reducing fields from 4 to 3 improves conversion by almost 50%
